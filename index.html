About Us

Once again, TIT&S BHIWANI is hosting a Tech Fest, under the direction of the Birla Education Trust, the Institute was established in 1943 amid the haze of the Second World War as a result of the vision of the great businessman and visionary Padma Vibhushan Dr. Ghanshyam Das Birla. Read More...

All of the Institute's undergraduate and graduate programs are currently associated with Maharshi Dayanand University, Rohtak. TIT&S continues its journey well into the twenty-first century thanks to a robust and illustrious alumni network that spans business and academics around the globe, a committed and knowledgeable faculty offering top-notch instruction, frequent placements, and a committed administration and staff.
