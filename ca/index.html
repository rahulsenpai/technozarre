CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAM

About The Program
In the campus ambassador program, you will represent our fest, Technozarre, in your college community and encourage students to participate by highlighting the advantages of taking part in the fest. Campus Ambassador is a very reputed position as you will be representing your entire institute.
An Opportunity To Grow
Your task as the campus ambassador is very flexible and easy to do, ranging from providing information about Technozarre'23 to asking students to register for the fest using your referral code. By becoming the campus ambassador you will serve as a link between the students of your college and Technozarre'23.
It will help to boost your confidence and leadership skills. Your communication skills will also bloom extravagantly. The campus ambassador program will become an asset if you are a student looking for great learning and networking opportunities.
